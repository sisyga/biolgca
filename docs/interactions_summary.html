Built-in Interactions¶

Note

This section is under construction.

Interactions specify the behaviour of the LGCA. A timestep consists of an interaction step followed by a propagation step.

To specify your own interaction function, please see the tutorial (source code -> BioLGCA.ipynb, dedicated page planned). Built-in interactions can be found below.

Interactions for classical LGCA¶

Interaction functions and helper functions for classical LGCA with volume exclusion.

lgca.interactions.aggregation(lgca)

Aggregation interaction.

Parameters:
lgca: LGCA_1D or LGCA_Square or LGCA_Hex

LGCA instance that the interaction is applied to

lgca.interactions.alignment(lgca)

Rearrangement step for alignment interaction :return:

lgca.interactions.birth(lgca)

Simple birth process coupled to a random walk :return:

lgca.interactions.birthdeath(lgca)

Simple birth-death process coupled to a random walk :return:

lgca.interactions.chemotaxis(lgca)

Rearrangement step for chemotaxis to external gradient field :return:

lgca.interactions.contact_guidance(lgca)

Rearrangement step for contact guidance interaction. Cells are guided by an external axis :return:

lgca.interactions.disarrange(a, axis=-1)

Shuffle a in-place along the given axis.

Apply numpy.random.shuffle to the given axis of a. Each one-dimensional slice is shuffled independently.

Parameters:
anumpy.ndarray

The array to shuffle

axisint, optional, default: -1

Along which axis to shuffle a. The default is -1, which implies the last axis.

lgca.interactions.excitable_medium(lgca)

Model for an excitable medium based on Barkley’s PDE model. :return:

lgca.interactions.go_or_grow(lgca)

interactions of the go-or-grow model. :return:

lgca.interactions.go_or_rest(lgca)

Interactions of the go-or-grow model without birth and death, i.e. only the switch and random walk.

lgca.interactions.leup_test(lgca)

Go-or-grow with least-environmental uncertainty principle. cells try to minimize their entropy with the environment, by changing their state between moving and resting. resting cells can proliferate. all cells die at a constant rate. :return:

lgca.interactions.nematic(lgca)

Rearrangement step for nematic interaction :return:

lgca.interactions.persistent_walk(lgca)

Rearrangement step for persistent motion (alignment with yourlgca) :return:

lgca.interactions.random(/)
lgca.interactions.random_walk(lgca)

Shuffle config in the last axis, modeling a random walk. :return:

lgca.interactions.wetting(lgca)

Wetting of a surface for different levels of E-cadherin :type lgca: :param lgca: :return:

Interactions for identity-based LGCA¶

Interaction functions and helper functions for identity-based LGCA with volume exclusion.

lgca.ib_interactions.birth(lgca)

Simple birth process

lgca.ib_interactions.birthdeath(lgca)

Simple birth-death process with evolutionary dynamics towards a higher proliferation rate. Family membership of cells can be tracked by passing the keyword argument track_inheritance=True in get_lgca().

lgca.ib_interactions.birthdeath_discrete(lgca)

Simple birth-death process with evolutionary dynamics towards a higher proliferation rate

lgca.ib_interactions.go_and_grow_mutations(lgca)

Simple birth-death process with tracked family membership of cells. New families develop by mutations. If lgca.interaction_params[‘effect’] == ‘passenger_mutation’: no change in proliferation rate, but mutations found new families If lgca.interaction_params[‘effect’] == ‘driver_mutation’: evolutionary dynamics towards a higher proliferation rate

lgca.ib_interactions.go_or_grow(lgca)

interactions of the go-or-grow model. formulation too complex for 1d, but to be generalized.

lgca.ib_interactions.random_walk(lgca)

Particles are randomly redistributed among channels with the same probability for each channel. In combination with deterministic propagation it produces an unbiased random walk.

lgca.ib_interactions.trunc_gauss(lower, upper, mu, sigma=0.1, size=1)

Sample from a truncated Gaussian distribution. :type lower: :param lower: lower limit of truncation :type upper: :param upper: upper limit of truncation :type mu: :param mu: mean of the distribution :type sigma: :param sigma: standard deviation of the distribution :type size: :param size: desired sample size :returns: (array) of size size, samples from the described truncated Gaussian

Interactions for LGCA without volume exclusion¶

Interaction functions and helper functions for LGCA without volume exclusion.

lgca.nove_interactions.dd_alignment(lgca)

Rearrangement step for density-dependent alignment interaction including the central lattice site.

lgca.nove_interactions.di_alignment(lgca)

Rearrangement step for density-independent alignment interaction including the central lattice site.

lgca.nove_interactions.go_or_grow(lgca)

interactions (switch, reorientation, birth, death) of the go-or-grow model for volume exclusion free LGCA

lgca.nove_interactions.go_or_rest(lgca)

interactions (switch, reorientation) of the go-or-rest model for volume exclusion free LGCA go-or-rest is a go-or-grow model without birth and death

lgca.nove_interactions.random_walk(lgca)

Rearrangement step for random walk interaction
